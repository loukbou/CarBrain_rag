RECALL NOTICE - SAMPLE
======================

NHTSA Campaign Number: 23V-123
Date: March 15, 2023

Vehicle Information:
- Make: Toyota
- Model: Camry
- Year: 2018-2020
- Component: Fuel Pump

Problem Description:
The fuel pump may fail prematurely, causing the engine to stall without warning.
This increases the risk of a crash.

Remedy:
Dealers will replace the fuel pump free of charge.

Owner Actions:
Contact your local Toyota dealer to schedule service.
For questions, call Toyota Customer Service at 1-800-331-4331.
